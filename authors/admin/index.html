Ekaterina (Katja) Sirazitdinova

Research Engineer in Computer Vision and Machine Learning, Data Scientist @ NVIDIA

Hi! I am Ekaterina (or Katja). I am a research engineer focusing on computer vision and deep learning. During my PhD studies, I was mainly working on the tasks of image-based 3D reconstruction and tracking, and afterwards spent some time doing research in deep learning for medical image analysis. I work as a data scientist at NVIDIA now, helping customers to solve their computer vision and video analytics problems. I also specialize in machine learning inference optimization and deployment of AI models on data center GPUs as well as in embedded / edge devices.
